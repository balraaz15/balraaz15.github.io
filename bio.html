Photographed Photo Collection

Although I don't fancy taking my own photos but I love to take photos. Whenever I go to some place nice, I take photographs of those places and other fascinating things. I don't have fancy and expensive cameras and lenses but I take most of the photos with from my smartphone. My phone never disappoints me. As much as I love to take photographs, I also love editing the photographs to give the photos special effects. Although I am not quite good at editing, I try whatsoever and it makes me happy. Most of my photograps include the pictures of historical and cultural heritages. I also like to capture the nature and other interesting things I see on some occassions and special days. I also use Instagram a lot, post my pictures there and also see other amazing galleries of talented photographers and I try to learn from them.
